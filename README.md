# Netflix Content Trend Analysis 📊

## Overview
Analyzed 8,800+ Netflix titles to identify content trends, 
growth patterns, and genre preferences using Python.

## Key Insights
- 71% of Netflix content is Movies vs 29% TV Shows
- Netflix saw its biggest content growth between 2016–2019
- USA produces the most content, followed by India and UK
- International Movies and Dramas are the most popular genres
- Netflix adds the most content in July and December

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
Netflix Movies and TV Shows — Kaggle
